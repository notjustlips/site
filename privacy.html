Modlinska Lips Inc. dba notJUSTLIPS New York ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our clients' personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you engage with our services or visit our website.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information except under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in providing services (e.g., medical professionals, payment processors), bound by confidentiality agreements

  • Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access, to protect your personal and medical information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and review the information we hold about you

6. Retention of Information

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or to comply with legal obligations.
