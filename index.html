Our Founder

Precision, experience,
and a standard of care.

Olga Modlinska, RN, is an internationally certified aesthetic nurse injector, trained by top doctors across the USA, Russia, Turkey and Europe. She began her career in 2019, combining clinical techniques from multiple disciplines into a structured approach that became the foundation of notJUSTLIPS — focused on anatomy, precision, and results that look genuinely natural.

She founded notJUSTLIPS around one principle: results should be consistent, not accidental. The clinic's signature method ensures every injector — RN or NP — works with the same approach, the same standards, and the same eye for natural, structured outcomes.

Every RN and NP injector at notJUSTLIPS is trained in Olga's approach — selected for clinical precision, a deep understanding of facial anatomy, and a genuine commitment to ongoing education. The standard of care she established from day one remains at the core of every treatment the clinic delivers.
